# Dual Boot Guide
***
This Document Compiles a series of online guides designed to assist you in Dual Booting your Computer.

## Steps
1. Download a Copy of Ubuntu 22.04 LTS (Jammy Jellyfish)
   * [x86 Desktop Version](https://releases.ubuntu.com/jammy/ubuntu-22.04.5-desktop-amd64.iso)
   * [arm64 Server Version, no GUI, not recommended](https://cdimage.ubuntu.com/releases/jammy/release/ubuntu-22.04.5-live-server-arm64.iso) 
2. [Create a Bootable Media](https://kb.filewave.com/books/filewave-general-info/page/rufus-creating-bootable-usb-drives)
3. Partition your Drive
4. Load into your BIOS 
5. Select the Bootable Media 
6. Walkthrough the Ubuntu Install Process 
7. Reboot Into GRUB 
8. Select your desired OS
